About this application

Report Card Generator

This platform helps schools generate styled, ranked report cards from subject spreadsheets in a single workflow.

What This Site Does

A quick overview of the features and workflow.

Template Download and Upload

Provides a subject template, accepts multiple subject files, and validates required columns before processing.

Weighted Scoring and Ranking

Computes weighted final scores using ClassScore and ExamScore percentages and calculates overall and per-subject positions.

Design and Branding

Lets you choose report card template style, custom colors, and logo placement to match school identity.

PDF and ZIP Outputs

Generates per-student PDFs, a compiled PDF, and a downloadable ZIP package for easy sharing.
